# Project Strike — Sports Events Wien

A sports event company focused on underserved live sports experiences in Vienna. Working title: Project Strike.

## Core thesis
Vienna has almost no compelling live sports events. Boxing works. Football and tennis are hard (logistics, silence requirements). Beachvolleyball has potential in summer (Donauinsel Worldcup). The gap is real.

## Strategy
Build the image first in Vienna before scaling outside the city. Once the brand is established, people will travel to events — expanding geographically gets much easier. Trying to build outside Vienna before having a local reputation is the wrong order.

## Candidate formats
- Boxing events (best fit — already a scene in Vienna)
- Beachvolleyball (seasonal, Donauinsel has existing infrastructure)
- Vierschanzentournee / ski jumping (logistically complex but high appeal)
- Ski racing (complex travel, harder to own)
- VBC (mentioned, Harry involved?)

## Open questions
- Who is the target audience — young urban, sports enthusiast, or broader?
- What's the business model — ticket sales, sponsorship, brand partnerships?
- What does "building an image" concretely look like at the start?
- Is this a solo project or does it need co-founders with event/sports industry background?
