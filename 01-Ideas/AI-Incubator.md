# AI Incubator

Exploring what VC differentiation looks like in an AI-native world. Core question: how do you maximize reach to ideas and back the right ones when AI collapses the cost of building?

## The questions driving this
- How does VC differentiation work when AI lowers barriers to building for everyone?
- What's the optimal model for maximizing idea reach — a creativity fund? A full-stack builder studio?
- How will idea generation and creative work be valued in an AGI era?
- Full-stack builders (Satya Nadella's framing) eliminate which roles specifically — and which survive?
- What is the value of an idea when execution becomes commoditized?
- How do you frame "creativity" as an investable asset?

- Factory Berlin (Stefan Krause) — company builder for entrepreneurs AND creatives, explicitly bets on creativity > hard skills in agentic era → [[03-Projects/Blog/Blog]]
- Slow Ventures Creator Fund — invests in creators holistically, ~10% equity stake, bets on the person not the project → [[03-Projects/Blog/Blog]]
